What is the law?

Under the Employment Standards Act, volunteer internships in Ontario are legal if they meet the following criteria:

1. are part of a program approved by a secondary school board, college, or university
2. provide training for certain professions
3. meet the six conditions required for the intern to be considered a “trainee”
